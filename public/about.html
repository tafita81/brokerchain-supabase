About BrokerChain

We run a 24/7 sourcing and dispatch desk for urgent situations: storm damage, flooded facilities, failed HVAC, backup power, spill control, and critical parts for public infrastructure under rapid-purchase limits.

We are U.S.-ready for fast payment. We bank in USD (Mercury). We execute DocuSign agreements on the spot so nobody waits for “legal next week.” Stripe lets us lock inventory / dispatch immediately.

We also operate in the U.S. micro‑purchase lane. Purchases below the ceiling (targeting ~$15k) can often be carded same-day in emergency contexts. Our AI triage asks if the buyer is authorized under that cap right now.

Our autonomous engine scrapes public emergency demand, scrapes suppliers who can solve that demand, reaches out in natural American English, promotes qualified requests to “sale_ready”, and matches them to standby providers.
