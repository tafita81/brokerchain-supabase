Emergency Dispatch Exchange (EMERG-)

Roof leak after hail. Basement flooded. AC dead at 2am. Backup power needed now. We capture the emergency, qualify it like a human, and immediately route it to a local provider on standby.

How it works

  1. You tell us ZIP code, problem, urgency (1-2h / today / this week).
  2. We confirm if it's insurance-backed (roof, storm, water) or out-of-pocket.
  3. We lock in dispatch via Stripe and send you DocuSign terms so nobody wastes time.
  4. A local pro gets the ticket. You get service.

Why providers pay

Storm / roof / water mitigation calls in the U.S. can be worth thousands of dollars, and full roof insurance claims can run well above $10k-$20k+ when damage is severe. Getting the first call means getting the job. That's why emergency contractors pay to be first in line.
